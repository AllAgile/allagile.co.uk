Our Services

Are you looking for a way to transform your organization into a more agile, innovative, and customer-centric one? Do you want to unleash the full potential of your teams and individuals and help them deliver value faster and better? If so, you might be interested in our agile services. We provide very experienced and certified agile coaches, trainers, facilitators, and mentors who have helped many organizations across different industries and domains achieve agility at scale. We understand agility and Scrum Mastery at a deep level:

Facilitation Guiding people through

We help you design and run effective meetings, workshops, and events that foster collaboration, creativity, and alignment among your people. We have a range of facilitated events and workshops we can facilitate with your people. We use various facilitation techniques and tools to create a safe and engaging environment where everyone can contribute and learn from each other. We also help you facilitate agile events, to ensure they are productive and valuable for your teams.

Mentoring Working with your people

We provide you with one-on-one or group mentoring sessions where we share our knowledge, experience, and insights on agile topics, such as agile frameworks, principles, practices, tools, and techniques. We help you solve your specific challenges and questions, and guide you to find the best solutions for your context. We also help you develop your agile mindset and skills, such as self-organization, feedback, experimentation, and continuous improvement.

Our people have deep and varied experience working as Scrum Masters, and Coaches, and can work with you to build competency.

Scrum Builders Game

This is a one-hour game that helps people understand the effects of organizational culture, leadership style, and behaviours on agility. It is a great way to introduce agile concepts and values to your teams and stakeholders and to spark discussions about how to improve your agile maturity.

Challenge your assumptions, and see how agile teams behave under didn't organisational Conditions
